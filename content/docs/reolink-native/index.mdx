Use any Reolink camera with Scrypted, including older/unsupported models and battery cameras without a hub. Uses the native Baichuan protocol instead of HTTP.

> [!WARNING] Testing Phase
> The plugin is under testing due to the variety of Reolink devices. Report issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/apocaliss92/scrypted-reolink-native) or in the Discord Reolink channel.

## Features

- **Regular cameras** — Full RTSP/RTMP and native streams
- **Battery cameras** — Auto-disable prebuffer/snapshots to preserve battery
- **NVRs** — Full NVR support
- **Videoclips** — Opt-in with auto-download
- **Multifocal (Trackmix)** — Combined stream in PIP mode
- **Accessories** — Motion-Siren, Floodlight, PIR, Autotracking, Chime
